Authentic yet comfortable living experience just minutes from the famous Tanna Volcano

From the balcony of your home you are always aware of the presence of this majestic mountain.
Every few minutes you will hear the roar and feel the tremble of a minor erruption.

Living with the villagers

Your host Freddy will introduce you to many of the people in his village, including his brother who is the Chief, and his mother who is a fantastic chef.

Activities around the village and island

You are a short walk from five other villages, and it is also an easy walk to the base of the volcano. Freddy also offers day trips around the island, from hot springs to ceremonial dancing, you will always have plenty to do!
